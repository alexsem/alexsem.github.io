## ğŸ”§ Customization

1.  **Personal Info:** Edit the Hero section in `index.html` to update your name, title, and bio.
2.  **Avatar:** Replace `avatar.JPG` with your own profile picture.
3.  **Social Links:** Update the `href` attributes in the Hero section with your LinkedIn, GitHub, and Email.
4.  **Projects:** Modify the "Selected Work" section to reflect your own projects.
5.  **Contact Form:**
    *   Go to [Formspree](https://formspree.io/) and create a new form.
    *   Copy your unique Form ID.
    *   Update the `<form action="...">` URL in `index.html` with your new ID.
